Our Hebe's

Hebe’s are evergreen shrubbery that have a place in any New Zealand garden. They are low maintenance with a large range of species available with a range of sizes. Our team is here to help get you the perfect plant for your garden.
The three types of hebes we plan to sell:

  • Margaret Hebe
    A compact bush that grows lance-shaped spikes which bloom lilac-blue flowers. Height often doesn’t exceed knee height which is great for pathways/driveways.
  • Hebe Evenosa
    Ball like shrubs that grow box-like leaves. Sprouts small cone-shaped flowers that are usually snowy white in colour. Same size-ish to Margaret Hebe.
  • Hebe Odora
    A shrub that rows pretty upright, which blooms white glossy flowers at the end of each stalk. Taller than both the Odora and Margaret, usually under hip height.
